How to Play Bible Quiz

Welcome to the Bible Quiz! Test your knowledge about the Bible and earn money based on your score. Here’s how it works:

  • You will be presented with multiple-choice questions about the Bible.
  • For each correct answer, you will earn points which are translated into money (K). Each correct answer is worth K 0.5, and each incorrect answer will cost you K 0.3.
  • Answer as many questions as you can before time runs out. There’s a countdown timer to keep track of your time!
  • If you want to stop the quiz and submit your score, simply click the "Done" button.
  • The more correct answers you give, the higher your score will be. The score represents the amount of money you could win in the game!
  • The top scorer at the end of the week will win a special reward! The game is part of a weekly draw, so your score could earn you even more prizes!

Good luck, and may your knowledge of the Bible help you win! Remember, the more you play, the better your chances of winning the weekly reward!
